The Guild's Story

Quest Board exists for the person who has everything they need to start — except the belief that starting is possible.

It was built by someone who knew they were capable but had never finished anything of their own creation. Someone who froze at the door not from laziness or lack of skill, but from the weight of making decisions alone in the void with no one to catch them if they fell.

Someone who assumed there was a floor — a minimum of money, experience, or certainty required before beginning — when the floor was never there.

Quest Board removes the floor. It is the hand that holds while it teaches. A mentor that makes the first move with you, not for you — so that the win, when it comes, is yours.

You don't need more than you have. You just need the first move.
